🍼 The under-3 reality check

Your group is unusual: all four kids are 5 or under, and two are just 1 and 2. Most "family resort" marketing targets the 4–12 kids'-club sweet spot, which does nothing for a baby. The single biggest differentiator is whether a resort takes babies and toddlers into supervised care — so the adults get real downtime — versus only offering nice spaces where a parent must stay.
